# Bridget J. Duffy — App Implementation Plan

A guide for bringing the running app in line with the static mockup, section by section.

---

## Nav / Header

- **No announcement bar** — keep as-is; do not add the dark olive bar from the mockup
- **Nav background** — change to the lighter green used in the mockup; reduce height/padding to match the more compact mockup style
- **Active nav item** — remove the box/outline indicator on "PORTFOLIO"; match the mockup's plain highlighted style

---

## Hero Section

- **Copy** — keep app copy: "Made to last a lifetime" with eyebrow "HANDCRAFTED FURNITURE" (no "Est. 2009")
- **CTA buttons** — keep the existing 2 buttons ("START A COMMISSION", "VIEW THE WORK"); do not add a third
- **Hero placeholder graphic** — replace the flat golden rectangle + wood emoji with an SVG crosshair graphic on the golden background. Specifically: a large outer ring (thick circle stroke) in a muted golden/tan color, inside which sits a filled olive-green circle. Centered on that circle is a white crosshair (one horizontal line, one vertical line, meeting at a small filled white dot in the middle). The whole composition sits on the golden/amber hero background, with a faint large circle arc visible behind the ring suggesting a subtle background circle. The effect is like a surveyor's target or compass rose — precise and craft-inspired.
- **Height & spacing** — increase the hero section height and internal padding to match the more generous proportions in the mockup

---

## Featured Pieces Grid

- **Image placeholders** — replace all-golden + wood emoji placeholders with color-varied blocks matching the mockup (mix of golden/tan, sage green, and olive tones); remove the wood log emoji
- **Card layout** — remove the title, label badge, and dimensions below each card; display plain colored image blocks only, matching the mockup's minimal grid style
- **Section header** — add "RECENT WORK" eyebrow label above "Featured pieces" heading, and a "VIEW ALL WORK" link aligned to the right of the heading row, matching the mockup

---

## Commission Section

- **Step labels** — change from "01" to "Step 01" format to match the mockup
- **Step card text color** — ensure body text inside the green step cards is white, not grey/muted
- **Section background** — set the commission section background to the beige/tan color used in the mockup (slightly warmer/darker than the off-white used in the app)

---

## Testimonials

- **Quote formatting** — add opening and closing quotation marks around each testimonial quote; ensure text renders in dark color (not muted), matching the mockup
- **Star ratings** — increase the size and visual weight of the ★★★★★ stars to match the larger, more prominent golden stars in the mockup

---

## Footer

- **Newsletter widget** — do not add the email subscribe input; keep app footer layout as-is structurally
- **Tagline** — change tagline from "Handmade with love since 2009" to "Hand-crafted furniture"
- **Copyright year** — keep app's "2026" (do not change to mockup's "2025")
- **Footer nav alignment** — keep the mockup's center-aligned footer layout, but without the subscribe widget column
